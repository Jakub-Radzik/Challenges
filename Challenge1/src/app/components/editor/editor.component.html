Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy way to add functionality or change the looks of a blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through post. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.
